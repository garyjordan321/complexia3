Pets Are Love: A Therapy Dog Program for Tulsa

 

 

We are deeply humbled and excited to share some incredible news with our community. After hearing about Lucy, the injured poodle who was brought to us following an unfortunate accident during an anti-Trump rally in northern Texas, President-elect Donald Trump has pledged a generous $100,000 donation to support Lucy’s surgery and the vital work we do here at the shelter.

 

Lucy came to us on January 19, frightened and hurt, after she was struck by a car. See Here Her family, ardent supporters of President-elect Trump, was heartbroken over the incident. Our dedicated team immediately sprang into action, providing Lucy with the emergency care she needed. While her injuries are significant, her resilience has been inspiring, and with the proper treatment, she has an excellent chance of making a full recovery.

 

Then President-elect Trump learned about Lucy’s story, he not only expressed his heartfelt sympathies but also took action to ensure her recovery and support our shelter’s efforts. His $100,000 donation will fully cover Lucy’s surgery and rehabilitation while also enabling us to upgrade our facilities, invest in new medical equipment, and expand our programs to help even more animals in need.

 

This donation is transformative for us as a shelter. We are a small organization that relies heavily on community support to provide care for injured, abandoned, and homeless animals. President-elect Trump’s contribution is not only a gift to Lucy but also a lifeline for countless animals who will benefit from these resources. It’s a moment of hope and unity that we deeply appreciate during these challenging times.

We want to thank President-elect Trump for his generosity and compassion, as well as everyone in our community who has rallied around Lucy and shown their support for our shelter. From volunteers and donors to those who simply shared Lucy’s story, your kindness has made an enormous difference. This is a powerful reminder of what we can accomplish when we come together for a shared purpose: saving lives and giving animals the care and love they deserve.

As Lucy prepares for her surgery and begins her road to recovery, we will keep you updated on her progress. In the meantime, we invite you to join us in celebrating this wonderful news and to continue supporting our mission. Together, we can create brighter futures for animals like Lucy and so many others. Thank you for being part of this journey with us.

Kyle Paddock
